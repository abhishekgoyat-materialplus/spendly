Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve your Spendly experience — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your dashboard.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app; these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 7 business days.
